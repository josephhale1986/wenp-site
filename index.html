When Pepe? Now.

WENP is a fixed-supply meme utility built directly on the Pepecoin network. Orders are listed in small, transparent batches so everyone can see what’s circulating.

Get Pepecoin How do I buy WENP?

You can buy PEPE directly on pepecoin.org or on one of the listed exchanges (MEXC, CoinEx, XT, AscendEX, Dex-Trade, StakeCube). Then swap / trade for WENP on Nintondo.
