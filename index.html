Utility Across the Pepecoin Chain

Chain-wide building block

WENP is designed to be a simple, fixed-supply utility token that any builder on the Pepecoin network can plug into their project — not just a one-off meme or single-market experiment.

  • Use WENP as an in-game currency or score multiplier.
  • Gate access to tools, dashboards, or premium features.
  • Reward activity (mining, node ops, art, dev work) with WENP payouts.
  • Pair WENP with other PRC20-Pepinal tokens in future markets.

If it lives on the Pepecoin chain and can read PRC20-Pepinal balances, it can plug WENP into its logic.

For everyone, not just whales

The goal is to keep WENP usable and approachable for regular Pepecoin holders — clear supply, clear markets, and amounts that make sense for games, utility, and day-to-day use.

  • Low-friction amounts suitable for games and micro-rewards.
  • No hidden mechanics or surprise emissions.
  • Simple mental model: fixed supply utility on Pepecoin.
  • Open invitation for devs to build WENP-powered apps, bots, and tools.

WENP should feel like a shared building block the whole chain can use, not a gated club.
